para gliding

Paragliding is an exciting and adventurous air sport that allows a person to fly like a bird using a lightweight, free-flying, foot-launched glider aircraft. It is a popular activity among thrill-seekers and nature lovers, offering a unique experience of soaring through the skies and enjoying breathtaking views from above. Paragliding not only provides a sense of freedom and exhilaration but also helps in overcoming fear and building confidence.

Paragliding in Tripura is gaining popularity, especially in the scenic Jampui Hills, located in the North Tripura district. Known as the "Land of Eternal Spring," Jampui Hills offers a pleasant climate year-round, making it an ideal spot for adventure enthusiasts

Location Paragliding activities are centered around Vanghmun, the base village of Jampui Hills.


For bookings and inquiries about paragliding in Jampui Hills, you can contact the Tripura Tourism Department at +91 9366149041.

River Rafting / Boating

While Tripura's rivers and lakes offer scenic beauty, there is no widespread infrastructure or services for white-water rafting at this time. However, the state is gradually developing its adventure tourism sector, so it's possible that river rafting opportunities may emerge in the future.



For now, if you're seeking water-based adventures in Tripura, consider:

Kayaking and Jet Skiing: Available at Dumbur Lake and Rudrasagar Lake.

Houseboat Rides: Experience the serene waters of Neermahal and Rudrasagar Lake.
